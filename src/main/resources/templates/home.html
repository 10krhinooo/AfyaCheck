Frequently Asked Questions

Is the questionnaire really confidential and secure? +
Absolutely. We employ bank-level encryption to protect all data transmission. We do not collect any personally identifying information like names, phone numbers, or email addresses. Your responses are processed anonymously and securely, and we never share data with third parties.
How accurate is the STI risk assessment? +
Our assessment is based on established medical guidelines from WHO, CDC, and Kenya's Ministry of Health. However, it's important to understand this is a screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. The results help guide you toward appropriate testing and care - only actual STI testing can provide definitive results.
What happens after I complete the questionnaire? +
You'll immediately receive a personalized report with your risk assessment, recommended testing timeline, specific tests to consider, local healthcare resources, and educational materials. You can save or print this report for your records or to share with a healthcare provider.
How long does the assessment take? +
The questionnaire takes approximately 5-10 minutes to complete. We've designed it to be thorough yet efficient, asking only the most medically relevant questions needed for an accurate risk assessment.
Can I retake the questionnaire? +
Yes, you can retake the assessment as often as needed. We recommend retaking it when your circumstances change, after new sexual partners, or as part of regular sexual health monitoring (typically every 3-6 months for sexually active individuals).
Do you provide actual STI testing? +
AFYACHECK provides risk assessment and connects you with healthcare resources, but we do not conduct physical testing. We partner with verified, licensed clinics and laboratories throughout Kenya to help you access appropriate, professional testing services.
Is this service free to use? +
Yes, the AFYACHECK questionnaire and risk assessment are completely free. Our mission is to improve sexual health awareness and access to care. While the assessment is free, actual testing at healthcare facilities may have associated costs.
What if I'm under 18 years old? +
The questionnaire is available to users of all ages, and we provide age-appropriate guidance. However, we strongly recommend that minors consult with a parent, guardian, or healthcare provider about sexual health concerns and testing. Many clinics offer confidential services for young people.
