Frequently Asked Questions

Is my information really confidential? +
Yes, absolutely. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. All assessments are completely anonymous, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your privacy is our top priority.
How accurate is the assessment? +
Our assessment is based on current medical guidelines and has been developed in consultation with healthcare professionals. However, it's important to note that this is a risk assessment tool, not a diagnostic test. For definitive results, always consult with a healthcare provider.
Do I need to create an account? +
No account is required for the basic assessment. You can take the questionnaire completely anonymously. However, creating an account allows you to save your results and track your sexual health over time.
What happens after I complete the assessment? +
After completing the assessment, you'll receive personalized recommendations including which STI tests you might need, nearby testing facilities in Kenya, and information about treatment options. You'll also receive educational resources tailored to your situation.
Is this service free? +
Yes, the risk assessment questionnaire is completely free. Our mission is to make sexual health information accessible to everyone in Kenya. While the assessment is free, any testing or treatment at healthcare facilities would follow their standard pricing.
Can I take the assessment for someone else? +
The assessment is designed for individual use. However, you can share the link with others who might benefit from it. Each person should complete their own assessment for accurate personalized results.
